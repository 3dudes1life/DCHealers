What Is Reiki?

A gentle complementary wellness practice.

Reiki is a form of energy work often used to support relaxation, reflection, grounding, and a greater sense of personal balance. A session may include quiet rest, intention setting, guided breathing, and hands-on or hands-off energy work depending on the format and client preference.

Every person experiences Reiki differently. Some people report feeling deeply relaxed, emotionally lighter, warm, calm, or simply more present. No specific belief system is required.

Reiki is a complementary wellness practice. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure medical or mental health conditions and is not a replacement for professional healthcare.

Frequently Asked Questions

What to know before a session.

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a complementary energy-based wellness practice often used to support relaxation, grounding, reflection, and personal balance.

How does distance Reiki work?

Distance Reiki is offered through a private video call. Daniel will guide you through preparation and intention setting before the energy-work portion of the session.

Do I need to believe in Reiki?

No specific spiritual or religious belief is required. Clients are welcome to approach Reiki with openness, curiosity, or a simple desire to relax.

What happens during an in-person session?

You will remain fully clothed and may sit or lie down. Daniel will explain the process and confirm whether you prefer hands-on or hands-off Reiki before beginning.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to relax. You may also want to bring water and avoid scheduling anything rushed immediately afterward.

Is video required for distance Reiki?

Final session procedures will be confirmed when bookings open. Video may be used for the opening and closing conversation even if you prefer to rest off camera during the energy work.

Can Reiki replace medical or mental health treatment?

No. Reiki is complementary and should never replace medical care, mental health treatment, medication, diagnosis, or emergency services.

Where will in-person sessions be offered?

In-person sessions are offered in the Escondido and North County San Diego area. Exact location information will be shared privately with confirmed clients.

Will international clients be able to book?

Yes. Distance sessions are planned for worldwide availability, subject to Daniel’s calendar and time-zone scheduling.

What happens if technology fails?

A technology and rescheduling policy will be published before bookings open. Clients will receive clear instructions with their appointment confirmation.

Important Wellness Disclaimer

Reiki is a complementary wellness practice and is not medical treatment, mental health treatment, diagnosis, or emergency care. Results and personal experiences vary. Always seek appropriate licensed professional care for medical or mental health concerns.
