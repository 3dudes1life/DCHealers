Frequently Asked Questions

Answers before your session.

How is a distance Reiki session held?

The session is held through a private video call. Daniel begins with a short conversation and intention setting before the Reiki portion of the session.

Can I receive distance Reiki outside California?

Yes. Distance Reiki is available worldwide, subject to scheduling and time-zone availability.

What do I need for a distance Reiki session?

Choose a quiet space, wear comfortable clothing, keep water nearby, and allow uninterrupted time to relax.

Do I need to remain on camera?

Session procedures are confirmed in advance. Video may be used for the opening and closing conversation while clients may prefer to rest off camera during the Reiki portion.
